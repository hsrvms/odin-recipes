Spicy Vegan Carrot Soup

Ingredients

  • 8 carrots, chopped
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon red curry paste
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro

Directions

Step 1

Combine carrots, vegetable broth, coconut milk, onion, garlic, curry paste, salt, and pepper in a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant PotÂ®). Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 15 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.

Step 2

Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, 10 to 40 minutes. Unlock and remove lid. Blend soup with an immersion blender until smooth and creamy, about 3 minutes. Serve in bowls and sprinkle with fresh cilantro.
